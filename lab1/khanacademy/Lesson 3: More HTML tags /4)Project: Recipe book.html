Shrimp Ramen

  • Time: 40 mins
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
yellow onion 2
fresh ginger 1 tablespoon
vegetable broth 6 cups
fish sauce 1/3 cup
raw shrimp 1 pound
mushrooms 1/2 cup sliced

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a Dutch oven over medium heat; add onions, carrots, ginger, lemongrass, and garlic, and sauté for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in chopped collard greens and sauté for 3 to 5 minutes.
  2. Add vegetable broth, soy sauce and fish sauce. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, and cook for 10 to 12 minutes.
  3. Add ramen noodles, and cook for 3 minutes. Remove from heat, add shrimp and mushrooms, and cover. Allow the heat from the broth to cook shrimp until pink and opaque, 5 to 7 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

Carrot Salad

  • Time: 2 hrs 20 mins
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
shredded carrots 4.5 cups
pineapple 8 ounce
sugar 0.5 cup
mayonnaise 0.5 cups

Steps:

  1. Combine carrots, pineapple with juice, raisins, sugar, mayonnaise, and lemon juice in a large bowl; mix well.
  2. Refrigerate for 2 hours before serving for flavors to meld for best results.

Basic Burger

  • Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
egg 1
beef 1 pound
bread crumbs 0.5 cup
black pepper and salt 0.5 teaspoon

Steps:

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate. Whisk together egg, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl.
  2. Add ground beef and bread crumbs and mix with your hands or a fork until well blended. Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties.
  3. Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
