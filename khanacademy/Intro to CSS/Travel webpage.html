Travel to Barcelona

Top sights in Barcelona:

  1. La Sagrada Familia


    2. The Basílica i Temple Expiatori de la Sagrada Família, otherwise known as Sagrada Família, is a church under construction in the Eixample district of Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. It is the largest unfinished Catholic church in the world.

    Designed by Catalan architect Antoni Gaudí (1852–1926), his work on Sagrada Família is part of a UNESCO World Heritage Site. On 7 November 2010, Pope Benedict XVI consecrated the church and proclaimed it a minor basilica.

  2. Spotify Camp Nou


    3. Camp Nou, branded as Spotify Camp Nou for sponsorship reasons, is an under-renovation stadium home of La Liga club Barcelona since its opening in 1957.

    With a planned seating capacity of 105,000, it will be the stadium with the largest capacity in Spain and Europe, and the second largest football stadium in the world.
